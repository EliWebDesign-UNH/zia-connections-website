Common questions

If yours isn't here, just ask — we respond within one business day.

Do I own my website?

The domain is always yours. The website content (text, images, your business info) is yours. We hold the underlying code and platform during the subscription — that's how we're able to maintain it for you. If you cancel, we'll provide an export of all your content in standard formats and help you transition to another provider or take it in-house. No ransom holds.

What happens if I cancel?

Email us. We honor month-to-month cancellation with no fees. Your site stays live through the end of the paid period, then we provide a 30-day window to export your content or migrate to another host. We don't make cancellation hard, and we don't make it awkward.

How long does it take to launch?

Most sites launch within 14 days of kickoff. The timeline depends on how fast you can provide content (text, photos, logo) and how many revision rounds you want. We're fast, but not at the expense of getting it right.

What if I already have a website?

We'll audit it for free and tell you honestly whether it needs a rebuild or just a tune-up. If your current site is fine, we'll say so. If it needs work, we'll show you exactly what and give you a fixed quote — not a "starting at" price that doubles later.

Can I make changes to my own site?

Yes — but most of our clients don't, because we handle it for them. The Growth and Pro plans include content updates, so you just text or email what you need changed. If you'd rather log in and edit yourself, we can set that up too. No markup fees for "client edits."

Do you do e-commerce / online stores?

Yes, on the Pro plan. We integrate with Stripe, Square, and Shopify. For simple product catalogs (under 50 SKUs), we build it directly into your site. For larger stores, we usually recommend Shopify and we manage the integration.

What if I need something custom?

Tell us. We build custom features on top of the standard plan — booking systems, review widgets, custom forms, third-party integrations. We'll scope it, quote it as a fixed price, and only start work after you approve.

Why should I trust a new agency?

Fair question. We don't have a 10-year track record yet. What we have: transparent pricing, no long-term contracts, a working example of the process (this site), and a real person (Eli) who answers your email. If we're not earning our keep, you fire us. That's the test.
