Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Who we are

Zia Connections is a Website-as-a-Service agency based in Albuquerque, New Mexico. We are a division of Unified Network Holdings. Our website address is ziaconnections.com.

What we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your business name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your current website URL (optional)
  • A hashed identifier derived from your IP address (not the IP itself)
  • Your browser user agent (truncated to 300 characters)

We do not collect your exact IP address for storage. We do not set tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party trackers.

Why we collect it

We use this information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry (within 1 business day, as promised on the form)
  • Provide the free website audit you requested
  • Contact you about services that may help your business

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Ever.

How long we keep it

We retain contact form submissions for as long as you remain a customer or until you ask us to delete them. We retain a hash of your IP for 30 days for spam prevention, then it is permanently deleted.

Where it is stored

Form submissions are stored in a file on our application server (hosted by Railway, Inc.) and logged to our application logs (also hosted by Railway). Our application server is located in the European Union region of Railway's infrastructure.

Email correspondence, once initiated, is stored with our email provider (Google Workspace) under the controls Google provides.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Show you what we have stored about you
  • Correct any incorrect information
  • Delete your information entirely

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@ziaconnections.com from the email address you submitted. We will respond within 7 business days.

How we protect your data

We use HTTPS (TLS) for all traffic. Form submissions are protected by a strict Content-Security-Policy, rate limiting (5 submissions per IP per hour), and input validation. We do not embed third-party scripts, fonts, or trackers that could exfiltrate your data.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted a form, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a material way, we will post the change here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
